Hearing loss, which is directly related to the level of sound a person can perceive, is the definition of deafness. This relates to the frequency of sound that a person may or may not be able to hear. Some people can only hear loud or soft sounds within a certain range, while others suffer from complete deafness. The condition of the inner ear and whether the problem has been present since birth or whether hearing loss developed much later are factors that affect a person’s ability to hear.

Mission and Vision

Mission:
To establish and develop the profession of sign language interpretation according to international standards, in partnership with all relevant institutions in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and abroad.

Vision:
To elevate the profession of sign language interpreters to be on par with interpreters of other languages and to apply the highest standards of quality in this profession.
